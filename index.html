EARLY BIRD

There will only ever be 1420 Early Birds in our genesis collection. Among the 1420 Early Birds will be 69 Legendary 1/1 Early Birds. Legendary Early Bird holders are offered exclusive, redeemable items in our Worms Mart (marketplace). Upon mint there will only be 23 Legendaries in our collection. Once per week for 23 weeks, we will transform a random holder’s regular Early Bird into a Legendary Early Bird live on Twitch. Along with these 23 Legendary Transformations, will be 23 weekly auctions for Legendary Early Birds. Legendary auction proceeds will go to the Bird Bank. We do not have plans for a second collection.
