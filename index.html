Early Birds Genesis

There will only ever be 1420 Early Birds in our genesis collection. Among the 1420 Early Birds will be 69 Legendary 1/1 Early Birds. Legendary Early Bird holders are offered exclusive, redeemable items in our Worms Mart (marketplace).
Upon mint, there will be 23 Legendaries in our collection. Once per week, for 23 consecutive weeks, we will transform a random holder’s regular Early Bird into a Legendary Early Bird, live on our Twitch.
Alongside these 23 transformations will be 23 weekly auctions for Legendary Early Birds. Proceeds will go to the Bird Bank.

ROADMAP

Hatch

Hatch our 1420 genesis Early Birds on the Ethereum Blockchain and begin our flight into the metaverse. Our mint date is scheduled for 5/31/2022 10am EST, at a price of .1 Ethereum per Early Bird.

Bird Bank (Treasury)

The Bird Bank will fund expansion of our utilities/roadmap, cover project expenses, and permit airdrops for holders. Owned and operated by Early Birds’ Founder and Treasurer- Chris Maffei.

$WORMS

Early Birds’ core utility token will be generated passively by holding Early Birds in your Metamask Ethereum wallet. Early Birds will generate 5 $WORMS each per day. You will be able to claim $WORMS when you want to redeem them.

Worms Mart

Early Birds will gain access to Worms Mart, Early Birds’ marketplace. Our unique marketplace will provide a wide range of redeemable items and services for holders. There will be Legendary Items, which are offered only to Legendary Early Bird holders and provide exclusive opportunities.

Bird Merch

Spread the good word of the bird via exclusive merchandise drops. Merch will be limited to 420 per item and will ship worldwide. Merchandise sales will directly support our artist, as well as power our Bird Bank.

Expand Utilities

We will continue to expand utilities for our holders and partner with pioneers in the Web3 space. We aim to create scalability through our expansions of utilities, partnerships, and solidification of our blue chip community/ demographic.

UTILITIES

Alpha

Early Birds Genesis is an expansive information hub, primarily composed of blue chip NFT holders. Our Alpha Chat, which is exclusive to Early Birds holders, is full of renowned alpha callers and engaged community members. We will continue to expand our team of alpha callers, with Chris at the head of the Alpha Department

Education and Community Building

Our provision of education in the Web3 space is one of our most beneficial utilities. We have a low supply with the intention of embracing our holders with a tight-knit community feel. This low supply also enables Chris to be engaged with every member, taking their personal situations and experience into account when addressing holder questions, ideas or concerns. This establishment of lasting relationships between holders promotes member retention and brand longevity.

Airdrops

Holders who follow specific guidelines will be eligible for random airdrops consisting of NFTs, Ethereum, and more!

Worms Mart

Holders will gain access to Worms Mart, Early Birds’ marketplace. Our unique marketplace will provide a wide range of redeemable items and services for holders. Only $WORMS are accepted.
